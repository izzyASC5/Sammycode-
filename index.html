Sammy

Who Am I?

Hi, I am Sammy Salama. Even though I am only only 15 years old, I'm a professional singer. I can sing in different languages like Italian, Hebrew and Arabic. I love music and simply couldn't imagine my life without it. As the youngest one of four children, no one ever listened to me, except when I sang. The only time I ever truly felt heard is when I am singing. I started singing from the time I could speak. I even use singing to help me study and complete my school work by writing songs about different subjects I am studying. In the future, I hope to produce my own music and travel the world on tour.

My Education

I am currently a freshman at The Professional Performing Arts School in New York, New York. I am currently on the school's honor roll.

My Performances

I have been part of multiple music groups and performed around the world. Some of my more favorite performances were at the Vatican in Rome, Italy, the Vienna Opera house in Vienna, Austria and Carnegie Hall in New York, New York.
